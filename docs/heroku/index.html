Heroku

Heroku is a great platform for simple, instant ruby app deployment.

Adding Exceptional to your Heroku rails apps is simple. Within the Heroku add-ons page choose the Exceptional add-on link.

You can also use the heroku command line tool to enable exceptional add-on for your heroku rails apps
